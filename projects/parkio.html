As part of my academic journey at C.V. Raman Global University, I developed an ATM Management System, which I documented in a detailed case study. This project was undertaken during my 4th semester in the Artificial Intelligence & Data Science branch. The ATM Management System is designed to provide a comprehensive solution for banking institutions to manage their ATM operations efficiently.

The ATM Management System is a computer-based application that centralizes the monitoring, control, and tracking of ATM activities. This includes managing transaction details, cash replenishment, maintenance schedules, and other critical functions. The system is designed to reduce downtime, enhance security, and improve customer satisfaction by ensuring reliable access to banking services.

This project was a significant learning experience, allowing me to apply my theoretical knowledge to a real-world problem. The guidance of my professors, Dr. Prashant Kumar Das and Ms. Jayanti Rout, was invaluable in completing this project successfully. The ATM Management System case study showcases my ability to design and implement efficient database solutions and highlights my commitment to leveraging technology to solve practical challenges in the banking industry.
